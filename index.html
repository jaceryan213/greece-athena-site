About Greece Athena

Welcome to Greece Athena Middle School, where tradition meets excellence. Located in Rochester, NY, we are proud to be the home of the Trojans — a community of learners, leaders, and innovators. Our school blends rigorous academics with vibrant extracurriculars, all rooted in a legacy of pride and achievement.

Whether you're exploring our math programs, joining a club, or cheering on our teams, Greece Athena is where your journey begins. Step into a space where gold shines brightest against the backdrop of bold ambition.
